## Health Benefits

### Walking 48 min/day (this route)
- **Calories burned:** ~200 kcal per trip (~100,000 kcal/year)
- **Steps:** ~5,300 per trip (~2.65 million/year)
- **Weekly exercise:** 240 min/week of moderate cardio
- **Meets WHO recommendations:** Yes (150 min/week minimum)
- **Health benefits:** Reduced cardiovascular risk, improved mood, better sleep, stronger bones

### Biking 16 min/day (this route)
- **Calories burned:** ~80 kcal per trip (~40,000 kcal/year)
- **Weekly exercise:** 80 min/week of vigorous cardio
- **Meets WHO recommendations:** Partially (75 min/week vigorous minimum)
- **Health benefits:** Improved cardiovascular fitness, lower body strength, joint-friendly, lower stress

### Driving 0 min/day
- **Exercise:** 0 min — contributes to sedentary lifestyle
- **Risks:** Associated with higher BMI, cardiovascular risk, and mortality
- **Boulder context:** Boulder County has the lowest obesity rate in Colorado (~18%) — active transport helps maintain this

### Mental Health Impact
- 🚶 Walking: Reduces anxiety by ~15%
- 🚴 Biking: Reduces stress hormone (cortisol) by ~20%
- 🚗 Driving: Associated with higher road rage and frustration

## Financial Analysis

### Per-Trip Costs
| Mode | One-Way Cost | 250-Day Annual Cost |
|---|---|---|
| 🚶 Walking | **$0** | **$0** |
| 🚴 Biking (own bike) | **$0** | **~$100–$200** (maintenance) |
| 🚴 Biking (B-Cycle) | ~$6.40/day | **$1,600/year** |

| 🚗 Driving | ~$4–$5/trip | **$1,000–$2,500/year** |
| 🚌 Bus (RTD) | ~$3.50 | **$1,750/year** |

### Hidden Driving Costs
- Gas: ~$1.20/round trip × 250 = $300/year
- Insurance: Average Boulder auto insurance $1,800/year
- Parking: Downtown Boulder $15–$25/month × 12 = $180–$300/year
- Maintenance: ~$500–$800/year
- Depreciation: ~$0.10/mile × 75,000 miles/year = $7,500/year (vehicle)

### Net Savings (vs. Driving)
- 🚶 Walking: **$1,000–$2,500/year**
- 🚴 Biking (own): **$800–$2,300/year**
- 🚴 B-Cycle annual: **$850–$2,350/year**
- 🚌 Bus: **$650–$750/year**

## Commitment & Action Plan

### Suggested Graduated Approach
1. **Week 1–2:** Bike 1 day/week + drive 4 days (test the route)
2. **Week 3–4:** Bike 2 days/week + drive 3 days
3. **Month 2:** Bike 3 days/week + drive 2 days + walk 1 day for variety
4. **Month 3:** Bike 4+ days/week + walk on nice weather days
5. **Optional:** Commit to "Bike-to-Work Wednesdays" as a standing habit
