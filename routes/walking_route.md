# 🚶 Walking Route: Detailed Guide

**Address:** 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway  
**Total Distance:** 4.03 km (2.5 mi)  
**Total Time:** ~48 minutes  
**Elevation:** Flat (~15 m total gain)  
**Calories:** ~200 kcal  
**Steps:** ~5,300

---

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

### 1. Start: 45 Oak Avenue
📍 Head **south** on Oak Avenue  
🚶 96 m | ~1.9 min

> Oak Avenue is a quiet residential street with beautiful American Elm canopy. This is the most peaceful segment of the entire walk.

**🏠 Remaining:** 3,930 m

---

### 2. Turn Right onto 19th Street
📍 Turn **west** (right) onto 19th Street  
🚶 2,157 m | ~25.5 min

> This is the longest segment. 19th Street is a major North-South arterial road with RTD bus routes 1, 3, 7, and 10 running frequently. Use the crosswalks at every intersection — Boulder is very strict about pedestrian right-of-way.

**🤝 Landmarks:**
- The street gradually slopes south toward Boulder Creek
- View of CU Boulder and the Flatirons to the west
- Several Colorado-style mansion gardens along this stretch

**📌 Safety:** Moderate traffic. Stay on the sidewalk. Watch for cars turning right across crosswalks.

**🏠 Remaining:** 1,773 m

---

### 3. Turn Right onto 20th Street
📍 Turn **north** (right) at 20th Street  
🚶 672 m | ~8 min

> 20th Street is a quieter residential cross-street. Walk along the north side for better views of the creek.

**🤝 Landmarks:**
- University Hill neighborhood — charming with local bookstores and cafes
- You will pass Heil House — a historic venue on 13th Street (if you accidentally continue straight)

**💡 Tip:** If you enjoy the vibe here, stop at Lucky 's Bakehouse Cafe (3980 Broadway) — one of the best brunch spots in Boulder. It is only 650m from 19th Street.

**🏠 Remaining:** 1,101 m

---

### 4. Turn Left onto Pearl Street
📍 Turn **west** (left) onto Pearl Street  
🚶 216 m | ~2.6 min

> Pearl Street is the historic Pearl Street Mall — Boulder 's famous pedestrian shopping street. This is a dismount zone: you must walk your bike on this segment.

**🤝 Landmarks:**
- Pearl Street Mall with local boutiques and street performers
- Rio Grande Mexican restaurant (1101 Walnut)
- Japango Asian restaurant (1136 Pearl)

**📌 Safety:** Very pedestrian-heavy. Walk your bike on the mall path. Bike racks are available at Walnut Street.

**🏠 Remaining:** 884 m

---

### 5. Turn Left onto 18th Street
📍 Turn **north** (left) onto 18th Street  
🚶 249 m | ~3 min

> A short residential connector street. Quiet and easy.

**🤝 Landmarks:**
- Near University Hill elementary school
- Small family-run businesses

**🏠 Remaining:** 635 m

---

### 6. Turn Right onto Canyon Boulevard
📍 Turn **east** (right) onto Canyon Boulevard  
🚶 637 m | ~7.6 min

> **This is the scenic highlight!** Canyon Boulevard runs along Boulder Creek with stunning views of the canyon walls and turquoise water. The path is wide and well-maintained.

**🤝 Landmarks:**
- Boulder Creek — watch for canyon visitors, swimmers, and tubers in summer
- Boulder Falls — visible from the path (a short detour)
- Mature cottonwood trees providing shade

**📌 Safety:** Steady sidewalk. The creek path is shared with cyclists and joggers — stay aware.

**🏠 Remaining:** 0 m

---

### Arrive: 1800 Broadway
📍 You have arrived!  
🎯 1800 Broadway is just east along Broadway from the Canyon Blvd intersection.

## Seasonal Tips for Walkers

### 🌸 Spring (April–May)
- Gorgeous wildflowers along Canyon Blvd
- Mornings can be chilly (50°F); bring a light layer
- Creek is running high from snowmelt — beautiful but slippery edges

### ☀️ Summer (June–August)
- Best walking weather (75–85°F)
- Canyon Blvd can get crowded with cyclists and tourists
- Stay hydrated — carry water; there are fountains along the path
- Sunrise walk = less crowded; sunset walk = gorgeous golden hour

### 🍂 Fall (September–November)
- Peak foliage along the creek corridor — golden aspens!
- Mornings cooler; afternoons pleasant
- Perfect season for this commute

### ❄️ Winter (December–March)
- Possible ice/snow on sidewalks — microspikes highly recommended
- Shorter daylight: consider starting later
- Snow removal is good on 19th Street; slower on residential streets
- Average high: 45°F; low: 22°F
