### Bike Infrastructure Highlights
- 🚴 **Canyon Boulevard** — The gem of this route! A **separated bike lane** runs along Boulder Creek with physical barriers from traffic. This is one of Boulder's premier cycling corridors.
- 🚲 **19th Street** — Designated bike route; watch for right-hook turns from cars
- 🚶 **Pearl Street Mall** — Dismount and walk your bike on the pedestrian mall section; bike racks available near Walnut Street
- 🆔 **B-Cycle Station** — Boulder's bike-share system has stations near both endpoints; you could ride a shared bike the entire route

### E-Bike Consideration
- Boulder's **flat terrain** makes this route ideal for a Class 1 e-bike
- **$1,200 Colorado E-Bike Rebate** can offset the cost
- Even with assist, you will still get 15–20 min of light exercise
- E-bike users may shave 3–5 additional minutes off the 16-min time

### Safety Notes
- Canyon Blvd bike lane is **physically separated** — the safest segment
- 19th Street requires extra caution; use the designated bike route
- Pearl Street Mall is pedestrian-heavy — dismount for safety
- Bright clothing + front/rear lights recommended for dawn/dusk commutes
